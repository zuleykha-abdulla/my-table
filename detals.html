TIME TO TALK

Ten months ago Simon found some old letters. They were from my old boyfriend, Geoff. My husband, Peter wasn’t Simon’s real father, you see. Geoff was Simon’s father, but he left me only a month or two before Simon was born. He didn’t want children. Then I met Peter. He was very good to me and fell in love. Soon after that, Peter married me. He was a good husband and good father to Simon. But Simon never knew the true story. After found Geoff’s letters, he was very angry with me. We didn’t talk for two days. Then Simon left home. That was ten months ago now. Where is he now? I have no idea. But I can’t live without him.

Then we heard the sound of coins.
